# **App Name**: FrameCraft Portfolio

## Core Features:

- Project Showcase Gallery: An interactive gallery for displaying video projects with thumbnails, titles, descriptions, and embedded video players (e.g., YouTube/Vimeo).
- Services Section: A dedicated page outlining 'JeevanEditz' video editing services, packages, and pricing structures.
- About 'JeevanEditz' Page: A page detailing background, experience, artistic vision, and any unique selling propositions.
- Blog with Articles: A platform for publishing articles related to video editing, industry insights, and creative processes.
- Contact Form: A submission form allowing visitors to send inquiries directly to 'JeevanEditz' and capture lead information.
- AI Blog Idea Generator: An internal tool to assist the editor in generating fresh and relevant blog post ideas based on recent industry trends or keywords.

## Style Guidelines:

- The visual theme is dark, drawing inspiration from a modern, ethereal lavender gradient often found in high-tech or creative spaces. The primary color, representing sophistication and creativity, is a vibrant lavender (#7F2DFF). This provides a compelling accent on the dark backdrop.
- The background color is a deep, desaturated purplish-grey (#26202B). This dark hue provides a rich contrast to interactive elements and textual content, evoking a sense of calm and focus.
- The accent color is a bright, analogous blue-violet (#8686FF), designed to draw attention to interactive elements, calls-to-action, and key highlights, creating a dynamic visual flow.
- Headlines use 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif), offering a modern, tech-infused yet professional aesthetic. Body text uses 'Inter' (sans-serif), chosen for its exceptional readability and neutral, objective feel, ensuring clear communication of service details and blog content.
- Utilize sleek, minimalist line icons that align with the professional and modern aesthetic of video production. Icons should complement the dark theme without being overly flashy.
- A clean, modular layout emphasizing visual content, particularly the video projects. Responsive design is key to ensure optimal viewing on all devices, with intuitive navigation to different sections.
- Subtle, smooth transition animations when navigating between pages or sections. Implement elegant hover effects on project thumbnails in the gallery to enhance user engagement.
